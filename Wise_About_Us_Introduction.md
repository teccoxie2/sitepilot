# SitePilot Media - Business Profile for Wise

## Registered Name
Xentech Limited (Operating as SitePilot Media)

## Website
https://sitepilot.co

## Business Description
SitePilot Media is a next-generation digital infrastructure authority and B2B technology media platform based in Auckland, New Zealand. We specialize in technical auditing, benchmarking, and performance analysis of global cloud infrastructure, AI-powered software-as-a-service (SaaS) platforms, and web development technologies.

## Nature of Business
Our primary business activities include:
1. **Technical Auditing Services**: We provide independent, data-driven security and performance audits for technology vendors looking to validate their infrastructure for enterprise clients.
2. **Affiliate Marketing & Lead Generation**: We generate revenue through strategic partnerships with globally recognized technology brands (e.g., Hostinger, Wix, SiteGround) by providing expert-led software recommendations.
3. **Strategic Consulting**: Assisting small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs) in selecting and deploying cost-effective, high-performance digital stacks.

## Target Market
Our audience and client base are global, primarily focusing on North America, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific region. We work with technology providers ranging from high-growth AI startups to established cloud infrastructure companies.

## Revenue Model
SitePilot operates a hybrid revenue model consisting of one-time technical audit fees (Standard Audit: $800 USD) and recurring commission-based performance marketing.

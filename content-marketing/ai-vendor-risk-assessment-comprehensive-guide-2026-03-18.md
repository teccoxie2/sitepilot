## Section 2: Framework Overview: 8-Dimensional Risk Assessment {#section-2}

### The Enterprise AI Vendor Risk Assessment Framework 2026

Our framework evaluates vendors across **8 critical dimensions**, each weighted based on enterprise risk tolerance and regulatory requirements:

#### **Dimension 1: Technical Architecture & Performance** (Weight: 15%)

#### **Dimension 2: Security & Data Protection** (Weight: 20%)

- **AI ethics and bias prevention measures**
- **Legal framework and liability structures**

#### **Dimension 4: Financial Stability & Pricing** (Weight: 12%)
- **Vendor financial health and funding status**
- **Pricing transparency and predictability**
- **Contract terms and SLA guarantees**
- **Total cost of ownership analysis**

#### **Dimension 5: Operational Excellence** (Weight: 10%)
- **Service availability and uptime track record**
- **Customer support quality and responsiveness**
- **Change management and communication**
- **Disaster recovery and business continuity**

#### **Dimension 6: Innovation & Roadmap** (Weight: 8%)
- **Product development velocity**
- **Technology roadmap alignment**
- **Research and development investment**
- **Market positioning and competitive advantages**

#### **Dimension 7: Governance & Transparency** (Weight: 10%)
- **Algorithmic transparency and explainability**
- **Audit trail and logging capabilities**
- **Governance structure and decision-making processes**
- **Stakeholder communication and reporting**

#### **Dimension 8: Strategic Partnership Potential** (Weight: 7%)
- **Cultural alignment and collaboration history**
- **Integration with existing enterprise systems**
- **Long-term partnership viability**
- **Knowledge transfer and training capabilities**

### Risk Scoring Methodology

**Scoring Scale:** 1-5 (1 = High Risk, 5 = Low Risk)  
**Weighted Score Calculation:** (Dimension Score × Weight) ÷ Total Weights  
**Risk Threshold Classifications:**
- **4.0-5.0:** Low Risk (Approved for critical business processes)
- **3.0-3.9:** Moderate Risk (Approved with monitoring)
- **2.0-2.9:** High Risk (Requires mitigation measures)
- **1.0-1.9:** Critical Risk (Not approved for enterprise use)

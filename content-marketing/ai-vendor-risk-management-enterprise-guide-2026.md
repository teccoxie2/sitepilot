## The Strategic Framework for AI Vendor Risk Management

### Phase 1: Pre-Selection Risk Assessment

#### **Vendor Due Diligence Framework**

**Technical Capability Assessment** (25% weight)
- Model accuracy and performance benchmarks
- Scalability and integration capabilities
- Technical architecture and infrastructure
- Innovation roadmap and R&D investment

**Security and Compliance Evaluation** (30% weight)
- Information security certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
- Regulatory compliance track record
- Data handling and privacy practices
- Incident response and recovery capabilities

**Financial Stability Analysis** (20% weight)
- Financial health and funding status
- Customer concentration and revenue diversification
- Insurance coverage and liability protections
- Long-term viability assessment

**Operational Excellence Review** (25% weight)
- Service level agreement performance
- Support quality and responsiveness
- Change management processes
- Business continuity planning

#### **Risk Scoring Methodology**

Implement a quantitative risk scoring system using a 100-point scale:

**Low Risk (80-100 points)**
- Established vendor with proven track record
- Comprehensive compliance certifications
- Strong financial position
- Minimal regulatory exposure

**Medium Risk (60-79 points)**
- Emerging vendor with limited track record
- Partial compliance certifications
- Adequate financial position
- Moderate regulatory exposure

**High Risk (Below 60 points)**
- Unproven vendor or technology
- Limited compliance capabilities
- Weak financial position
- Significant regulatory exposure

### Phase 2: Contract Negotiation and Risk Allocation

#### **Essential Contract Provisions for AI Vendors**

**Data Protection and Privacy**
- Explicit data processing limitations

- Data deletion and portability guarantees

**Intellectual Property Protection**
- Clear IP ownership definitions
- Confidentiality and non-disclosure terms
- Protection of proprietary algorithms
- Restrictions on model training data use

**Performance and Service Levels**
- Quantifiable performance metrics
- Service availability guarantees
- Response time commitments
- Remedies for performance failures

**Compliance and Regulatory Requirements**
- Adherence to applicable regulations
- Notification of regulatory changes
- Cooperation with compliance audits
- Liability for compliance violations

**Termination and Transition**
- Data extraction and migration rights
- Transition assistance requirements
- Intellectual property return
- Post-termination obligations

#### **Risk Allocation Strategies**

**Insurance Requirements**
- Professional liability: Minimum $5M coverage
- Cyber liability: Minimum $10M coverage
- Errors and omissions: Minimum $2M coverage
- Directors and officers: Minimum $5M coverage

**Liability Limitations**
- Cap indirect damages but preserve direct damage claims
- Exclude liability caps for data breaches and IP violations
- Require vendor indemnification for third-party claims
- Include regulatory fine sharing provisions

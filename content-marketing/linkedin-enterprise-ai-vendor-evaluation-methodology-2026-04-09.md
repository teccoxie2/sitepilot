# LinkedIn Draft — Enterprise AI Vendor Evaluation Methodology

Most enterprise AI buying processes do **too much comparison** and **not enough elimination**.

That is how weak vendors survive.

A decent enterprise AI methodology should answer seven ugly questions fast:
- Is customer data used for training or product improvement?
- Can the vendor support SSO, MFA, RBAC, and audit logs properly?
- Are export, deletion, and rollback paths real or hand-wavy?
- Does pricing still work after pilot-to-production scale?
- Which issues are pass/fail, not “we’ll figure it out later”?
- What must be proven in pilot instead of promised in demo?
- How do legal, security, architecture, and procurement score the same evidence?

# LinkedIn Draft — Enterprise AI Vendor Procurement Cluster

Most enterprise AI buying processes fail in a stupidly predictable way:

Teams compare demos, not operating reality.

So we built a tighter BOFU content cluster on SitePilot for procurement and architecture teams:

- Enterprise AI vendor comparison guide
- AI vendor due diligence checklist
- Enterprise AI vendor RFP template
- Vendor shortlist scorecard
- Vendor pricing guide
- Pilot evaluation checklist

The point is simple:

**Methodology -> Comparison -> RFP -> Due Diligence -> Scorecard -> Pilot**

That sequence forces buying teams to validate:
- security controls
- data governance
- pricing traps
- rollout risk
- exit terms

Before production approval.

A few hard truths:
- If a vendor cannot explain training usage clearly, that is already an answer.
- If pricing only works in slide decks, the TCO is fiction.
- If the pilot has no rollback criteria, it is not a pilot. It is pre-approved risk.

Enterprise AI pilots fail when teams treat them like extended demos.

A real pilot should do three things before contract signature:
- prove the tool works on live workflows, not canned prompts
- prove security and governance controls in the real environment
- prove the commercial model still makes sense under actual usage

If the pilot cannot disqualify a weak vendor, it is theater.

That is why we built a tighter evaluation path on SitePilot:
RFP → due diligence → shortlist scorecard → pilot evaluation → pricing review.

The goal is simple: turn unresolved claims into measurable evidence before production approval.

If you are buying enterprise AI in 2026, optimism is not a control.
Evidence is.

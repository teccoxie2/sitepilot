# LinkedIn Post — Independent AI Vendor Audit

Most enterprise AI buying processes still die in the same stupid place: teams compare vendor demos instead of comparing procurement evidence.

A serious AI vendor review should answer a few ugly questions before anyone talks rollout:
- Where does customer data go?
- Can legal verify training and retention terms?
- Does pricing still work after pilot usage becomes production usage?
- Can the team exit without rebuilding everything?
- Which controls are pass/fail, not "we'll sort it out later"?

We built SitePilot’s enterprise procurement cluster around that reality:
- vendor comparison
- RFP template
- due diligence checklist
- decision matrix
- shortlist scorecard
- pricing guide
- pilot checklist

The goal is simple: kill weak vendors early and document why the winner survives.

If your shortlist still depends on polished demos and sales decks, your process is not mature yet.

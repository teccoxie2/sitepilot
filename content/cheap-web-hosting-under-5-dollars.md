## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can $5 hosting handle business websites?
Yes, for small to medium businesses with moderate traffic (under 50k visitors/month). E-commerce sites should consider managed WordPress hosting or VPS options.

### What happens when I outgrow budget hosting?
Most providers offer easy upgrades to VPS or dedicated servers. Plan the transition when approaching resource limits.

### Are cheap hosts less secure?
Not necessarily. The providers we recommend maintain good security standards. However, you're responsible for keeping your website and applications updated.

### How much traffic can budget hosting handle?
- **Light traffic:** Under 10k visitors/month - any option works
- **Moderate traffic:** 10k-50k visitors/month - Hostinger or A2 Hosting
- **Heavy traffic:** 50k+ visitors/month - consider VPS upgrade

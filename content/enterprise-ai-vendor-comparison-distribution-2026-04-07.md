## Core angle
Buying teams should compare enterprise AI vendors using evidence from RFP responses, due diligence, and weighted shortlist scoring — not demo energy.

## LinkedIn post
Most enterprise AI vendor selections fail before the pilot even starts.

The problem usually is not the model. It is the buying process.

Teams compare vendors with fragmented criteria:
- procurement looks at price
- security looks at controls
- legal looks at clauses
- the business looks at speed

That creates fake alignment and expensive ambiguity.

I put together a practical 3-step evaluation path:
1. RFP template for initial filtering
2. due diligence checklist for real control review
3. shortlist scorecard for weighted final ranking

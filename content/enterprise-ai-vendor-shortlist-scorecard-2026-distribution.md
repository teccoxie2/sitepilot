## Scoring Dimensions
1. **Strategic Fit (15%)**: Alignment, industry expertise, roadmap.
2. **Technical Architecture (15%)**: Scalability, latency, benchmarks.
3. **Data Governance (20%)**: Training usage, residency, masking.
4. **Security & Trust (15%)**: Identity (SSO/RBAC), audit logging.
5. **Commercial Model (10%)**: TCO, pricing transparency, SLAs.
6. **Implementation Speed (10%)**: Time-to-value, support.
7. **Regulatory Compliance (10%)**: Privacy (GDPR), auditability.
8. **Risk Profile (5%)**: Portability, subprocessor transparency.

## Best Practice Summary
Assign a score from 1-5 for each dimension based on vendor evidence. A weighted average below 3.5 usually indicates significant remediation is required before a production rollout.
